FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What makes a cyber security developer different from a regular developer?

    Cyber security developers have specialized knowledge in security principles, threat modeling, and secure coding practices beyond standard development skills. They understand common attack vectors, security vulnerabilities, and how to implement defensive measures in code. While regular developers focus primarily on functionality and performance, security developers prioritize protecting applications from threats while maintaining those aspects.

  • How do you stay current with evolving security threats?

    Our team maintains continuous education through security certifications, participation in security communities, and subscriptions to threat intelligence feeds. We regularly review security bulletins from organizations like OWASP, NIST, and US-CERT. We also conduct internal knowledge-sharing sessions and participate in security conferences and workshops to ensure we're aware of emerging threats and countermeasures.

  • Can you secure our existing applications, or do we need to rebuild them?

    In most cases, we can significantly improve the security of existing applications without a complete rebuild. We start with a comprehensive security assessment to identify vulnerabilities, then implement targeted fixes and security enhancements. However, if an application has fundamental architectural security flaws or uses severely outdated technologies, we may recommend partial or complete redevelopment to ensure adequate security.

  • What compliance standards can your security implementations meet?

    Our security implementations can be tailored to meet various compliance standards including GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and CCPA. We have experience developing applications that satisfy regulatory requirements across healthcare, finance, e-commerce, and other regulated industries. Our team stays current with evolving compliance standards to ensure your systems remain compliant as regulations change.

  • Do you provide security training for our development team?

    Yes, we offer customized security training programs for development teams. These include secure coding workshops, threat modeling sessions, and security code review training. We believe in building security capacity within your organization, not just delivering secure code. Our training programs are hands-on and tailored to your team's specific technology stack and security needs.
