FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long does it take to develop a website?

    The timeline for website development varies depending on complexity, features, and content requirements. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex e-commerce site could take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific requirements.

  • How much does it cost to build a website?

    Website costs vary widely based on complexity, features, design requirements, and content needs. Simple informational websites may start around $3,000, while complex e-commerce or custom web applications can range from $10,000 to $50,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project scope.

  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    All websites we develop are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. We design and test on multiple devices and screen sizes to ensure your website looks great and functions perfectly regardless of how users access it. With mobile traffic accounting for over 50% of web traffic, this is a critical aspect of modern web development.

  • Do you provide website maintenance services?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive website maintenance packages to ensure your site continues to function optimally. This includes security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. Regular maintenance is essential to protect your investment and keep your website secure and up-to-date.

  • Will my website be SEO-friendly?

    Yes, we build all websites with SEO best practices in mind. This includes clean code, fast loading times, mobile responsiveness, proper heading structure, schema markup, and SEO-friendly URLs. We also provide guidance on content optimization and can implement additional SEO features to help improve your search engine rankings.
